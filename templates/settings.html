IMAP / SMTP Config

Configure these in your .env file, then restart the server. The tracker uses SMTP_USER and SMTP_PASSWORD (Gmail App Password) for IMAP access.

SMTP_USER Your Gmail address (e.g. you@gmail.com)
SMTP_PASSWORD Gmail App Password — generate one here
OPENAI_API_KEY Used for email classification (GPT-4o)
Gmail setup: Enable IMAP in Gmail Settings → See All Settings → Forwarding and POP/IMAP → Enable IMAP. Then create an App Password at myaccount.google.com/apppasswords (requires 2FA enabled).

Auto-Apply Agent

The apply agent uses headless Chrome (Selenium) to submit your tailored resume to job portals.

LinkedIn
Easy Apply only. Login required — log in to LinkedIn in Chrome first.
Naukri
Clicks Apply, uploads resume. Login required.
Indeed
Easily Apply jobs. Some jobs redirect to company site.
Glassdoor
Usually redirects to company ATS — marks as manual.
Note: If the bot is blocked by CAPTCHA or login wall, it marks the job as "Manual Apply Needed" and shows an "Open Job Page" button. Portal login sessions are not persisted between runs — for best results, log into portals in your default Chrome profile.

How It Works

  1. Upload PDF — paste job URL + upload resume PDF
  2. AI Analysis — agent scrapes JD, rewrites resume, generates cover letter
  3. Auto-Apply — click "Auto-Apply" on any application to submit via browser bot
  4. Download PDF — download the ATS-optimised resume
  5. Email Tracking — tracker monitors inbox, detects replies from hiring teams
  6. Timeline — full history per application: applied, interview invite, offer, rejection
